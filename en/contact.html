Location

Where are we?

Located in the Boulemane Province, Fez-Meknes Region, the Oumjniba village belongs to the rural commune of El Mers. It stands proudly at the foot of the majestic Tichoukt massif, 110km from the imperial city of Fez.

GPS Coordinates 33°19′39″ N, 4°41′07″ W
Average Altitude 1,931 meters (Tichoukt: 2,796m)
Zip Code 33102 — El Mers Commune
Administration Boulemane Province, Fez-Meknes Region
