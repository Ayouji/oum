Location

Where are we?

Located in the Boulemane Province, Fez-Meknes Region, the Oumjniba douar belongs to the rural commune of El Mers. It stands proudly at the foot of the Tichoukt massif.

GPS Coordinates 33°19′39″ N, 4°41′07″ W
Altitude 1,931 meters
Postal Code 33102 — El Mers Commune
