Local know-how

Economic activities

A mountain economy rooted in the land, livestock, and crafts, passed down through generations at the foot of Tichoukt.

🐑
Livestock
Herders & shepherds — sheep and goat flocks adapted to high altitude.
🍯
Beekeeping
Mountain honey, harvested from the wild flowers of the Middle Atlas.
🧶
Crafts
Berber weaving & rugs, a living heritage passed from mother to daughter.
🏔️
Tourism
Mountain tourism: hiking, snow, and ecotourism.
🌾
Agriculture
High-altitude crops, including the precious saffron — "red gold".
