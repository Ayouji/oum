Local Cooperative · Agriculture
Safran d'Oumjniba
Local Product

Production and promotion of Oumjniba saffron. Manager: Hafid Boutmazguida. A cooperative that highlights high-altitude agriculture in the Middle Atlas and Moroccan red gold.

Traditional Crafts
Tafrine Tichoukt Cooperative

Traditional crafts: carpet weaving, cutting, sewing, and fine embroidery. President: Fatima Ayoujil. An invaluable know-how, passed down from mother to daughter, which preserves a living heritage.

Associative Life

Active associations

The civil society of Oumjniba is remarkably well organized and committed to the social, religious, and cultural development of the village.

Vital services
Oumjniba Water Assoc.

Rigorous management, distribution, and daily maintenance of the village's drinking water network.

Social Development
Tichoukt Women's Assoc.

Empowerment of women in the region through crafts, literacy, and continuous training.

Social inclusion
Essalam Assoc. (Disability)

Support, guidance, and help with the integration of people with disabilities within the community.

Religious culture
Quranic Sciences Assoc.

Teaching, memorization, and dissemination of Quranic sciences among younger generations.
