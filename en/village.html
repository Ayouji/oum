Memory & Territory

The Village of Oumjniba

Discover what makes this village a unique place — its history and infrastructure.

Identity

A land sculpted by altitude

At 1,931 meters of altitude, Oumjniba is an exceptional territory that has forged a unique cultural and human identity.

Harsh Climate

One of the coldest climates in Africa, often cutting the village off from the outside world for entire weeks during winter.

Pastoral Expertise

Sheep farming and transhumance have structured the local economy for centuries, passed down through generations.

Amazigh Heritage

The Tamazight language, proverbs and oral traditions form an exceptional living heritage, nationally recognized.
