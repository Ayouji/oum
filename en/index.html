Welcome

A territory of character.

Oumjniba is not just a village. It is a place that has shaped people as much as people have shaped it.

Isolated during the long winter months, this Middle Atlas village preserves an authentic way of life, punctuated by the seasons, pastoral farming, and the centuries-old Amazigh traditions of the Aït Seghrouchen tribe.

  • An extreme mountain climate
  • A spirit of solidarity (Touiza)
  • A resilient local economy
